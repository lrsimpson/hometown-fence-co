Materials Choices

Aluminum

Aluminum is our most common type of fence. This type of fence will last longer than any of our other options, is maintenance-free, and comes is many different heights and styles. Aluminum is also the most common type of fence for pool fencing. If you are not concerned with adding privacy to your yard, aluminum is the way to go!

Wood

Wood can be used is multiple styles of fences. Our two most common wood fences are wood privacy and split rail. This is a more economic option for privacy or split rail fencing, but typically requires a little more maintenance over time. For advice on wood maintenance, contact us today or when you have us install your new wood fence!

Vinyl

Vinyl can also come in multiple styles, including vinyl privacy and vinyl picket in multiple configurations. This product will last longer than any wood privacy or picket applications and is relatively low-maintenance besides the occasional pressure wash. The privacy options for this come in white, tan, and mocha colors. Check out our distributor's website for more details!

***These are just our most popular material options. We install other types of fences, such as chain-link, as well. Email us today to find out other materials options for your new fence!

For all aluminum and vinyl products, we use Hudson Fence Supply which is based out of Fort Mill, SC. To see their product specifications and more details, visit www.hudsonfencesupply.com.
