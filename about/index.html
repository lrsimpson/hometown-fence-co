About Us

After spending years gaining experience in construction, specifically fence construction, we decided to take our passion to the next level by starting this company.  We have made it our goal to provide people with a contractor who they can trust to do honest work at a great price. We know it can be intimidating to move forward with projects like this, so we want to make it as easy on the customer as possible.

Whether it's a new dog or your kids, we know how helpful a fence can be in deterring them from leaving your yard. We also know how helpful a fence is in protecting your personal belongings such as grills or patio furniture. Let us help you protect those you love!
